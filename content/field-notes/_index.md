# Capture notes

Capture notes are the field journal of Mapping Tournai. They record where imagery was collected, how routes worked in practice, what caused difficulty, and what should be checked later in OpenStreetMap or QGIS.

These notes are not software changelogs. They connect the coverage figures to actual roads, weather, equipment, movement, and observations on the ground.
