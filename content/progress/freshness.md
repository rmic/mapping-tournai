Coverage alone does not show whether imagery still reflects current conditions. The current baseline classifies road length as:

- **268 km fresh**
- **54 km stale**
- **247 km outdated**
- **776 km with no nearby images**

The oldest valid capture date in the current data is **19 February 2015**. The newest is **30 June 2026**.

## Freshness map

Freshness is now shown directly in the unified [coverage quality map](/progress/coverage/): solid green/orange/grey lines identify fresh, stale, and outdated 360 coverage, while dashed lines show front-facing imagery with the same freshness colors.

Freshness helps distinguish roads that have never been captured from roads that may benefit from a new visit.
